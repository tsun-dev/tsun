What it is

  • CLI-first DAST scanner for small SaaS teams
  • Runs locally or in CI
  • Handles auth, baselines, and noise by default
Why it exists:

ZAP is powerful, but painful to run in CI. Rukn makes authenticated ZAP scans repeatable, debuggable, and sustainable for real teams.
